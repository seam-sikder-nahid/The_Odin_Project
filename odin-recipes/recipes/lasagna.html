Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian comfort food made with layers of pasta, rich meat sauce, and creamy cheese.

This hearty dish is perfect for family dinners and special occasions.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef
  • Marinara sauce
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Salt & pepper

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 190°C (375°F).
  2. Boil lasagna noodles until tender.
  3. Cook ground beef and mix with marinara sauce.
  4. Layer noodles, meat sauce, and cheese.
  5. Repeat layers and top with cheese.
  6. Bake for 45 minutes.
  7. Cool for 10 minutes before serving.
