Pancakes

Description

Pancakes are soft, fluffy breakfast treats loved by people of all ages.

They are quick to make and taste amazing with syrup or fruits.

Ingredients

  • All-purpose flour
  • Milk
  • Egg
  • Sugar
  • Baking powder
  • Butter
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Mix dry ingredients together.
  2. Add milk and egg, then mix well.
  3. Heat pan and melt butter.
  4. Pour batter onto pan.
  5. Cook until bubbles form, then flip.
  6. Cook until golden brown.
  7. Serve hot.
