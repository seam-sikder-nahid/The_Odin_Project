Chocolate Cake

Description

Chocolate cake is a rich and moist dessert loved worldwide.

This recipe creates a soft cake with deep chocolate flavor.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Cocoa powder
  • Sugar
  • Eggs
  • Butter
  • Baking powder
  • Milk

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C (350°F).
  2. Mix dry ingredients.
  3. Add eggs, milk, and butter.
  4. Mix until smooth batter forms.
  5. Pour into baking pan.
  6. Bake for 35 minutes.
  7. Cool before serving.
